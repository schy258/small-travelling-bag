As a passionate traveler, I have embarked on a remarkable journey, exploring over 20 states of India. From the bustling streets of Delhi to the Kanyakumari, each state has offered me a unique tapestry of culture, traditions, and breathtaking landscapes.

More About Me

locales I explored

During my adventurous journey, I delighted in the diverse locales I explored. From picturesque coastal towns to enchanting mountain villages, each new destination offered a captivating tapestry of sights and experiences.

Jammu & Kashmir
Undoubtedly, known as Heaven on Earth, this exotic state of India is popular for its divine beauty of hills
Himachal-pradesh
Known as the summer destination in India, this state is famous for its hill stations.this beautiful state is the stoppage for all bikers, skaters and rock climbers.
Uttrakhand
Home to many Hindu temples and pilgrimage sites. this irresistible hill station is also famous for trekking and camping.
Chnadigarh
Chandigarh also known as “The city beautiful” is one of the most well planned cities of india also a union territory that serves capital of both neighbouring states i.e. punjab and haryana.
Punjab
The land of five rivers, this charming state known for its warm hospitality. Home to Golden Temple and numerous religious sites.also referred to as the state of lassi and aloo paratha.
Haryana
known as 'The Home of Gods',state having bountiful culture,heritage, festivals, folklores.The rich Haryanvi culture is characterised by the hookahs and the khat & one of the wealthiest states in India
Rajasthan
The state that houses Thar Desert and innumerable royal palaces and forts,known for its royal culture and historical monuments, such as Hawa Mahal, and the numerous baoris.
Delhi
The city is well-known for authentic food and fashion. popular for Mughal architecture.having shopping markets in some of the historically places with its numerous hidden gems.
Uttar Pradesh
Home to the Taj Mahal also famous for religious and cultural places, such as Varanasi, Agra, Lucknow, Fatehpur Sikri, etc., and the production of leather goods and crops
Bihar
Bihar is the origin of the two biggest religions in the world, namely Buddhism and Jainism.With the World's Oldest University- "Nalanda"!
Jharkhand
Jharkhand is blessed with lush green forests, and a treasure of mineral reserves. It has many revered places of worship too, which make it an attractive destination.
West Bengal
famous for Sunderban forests, tiger reserves and breathtaking landscapes. Located in the eastern part of India,home to different civilisations and has a vast historical significance.
Madhya Pradesh
Also known as the heart of the country, Madhya Pradesh is rich in diamond and copper reserves. Famous for its Khajuraho temples, this elegant state is full of historical places and monuments.
Maharashtra
From Bollywood to the chawls of Mumbai, stunning beaches to ancient caves and the Ganesha festival to rich art and culture,known as the Land of Legendary Icons, such as Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj.
Goa
Well-known for beaches, which have golden sand and crystal-clear waters, are some of the most stunning in all of India. The exotic nightlife is another factor. renowned for seafood, traditional festivals and Portuguese colonial architecture.
Karnataka
Known for its Mysuru Palace and Bengaluru, the technology hub of India, Karnataka, is also famous for its unforgettable history dating back to thousands of years and delightful hill stations surrounded by peaceful nature.
Kerala
Known as "God's Own Country," is recognized backwaters including luxurious boat hotels, etc. Kerala boasts about its beaches like Kovalam and Varkala. Ayurveda, a traditional form of medicine, is well-known in Kerala. also known for its natural beauty, festivals, and authentic cuisine.
Tamil Nadu
"oldest living language in the world" home to several distinctively Tamil festivals, such as Pongal or Karthigai Deepam, that can only be experienced in the state.Ramanathaswamy Temple is a Hindu temple dedicated to the god Shiva located on Rameswaram island in the state of Tamil Nadu.
Pondycherry
Union Territory of India is a pristine French colony with enchanting villas that spellbind tourists with their architecture and is also referred to as the French Capital of India. "Auroville"a universal town where men and women of all countries are able to live in peace above all creeds, and all nationalities.
